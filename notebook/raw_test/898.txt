Trade war fears hammered global stock markets and saw the the FTSE 100 suffer its worst one-day decline in over a year.

London's main index tumbled 2.24 per cent or 172.43 points to 7,509.84 points. It was the largest one-day drop for the FTSE 100 since April 18 2017, when it fell 2.46 per cent.

Similar losses landed in Europe where the French CAC 40 and German Dax slumped 1.9 per cent and 2.4 per cent respectively.
