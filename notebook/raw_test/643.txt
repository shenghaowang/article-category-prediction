Wal-Mart plans to expand its video game trade-in program to its stores. The retailer plans to let video game owners trade in used games online and in stores for store credit but not cash. The credit can be used in Wal-Mart and Sam's Club stores.

The retailer had offered trade-ins on a more limited basis online.

It will offer refurbished used games in its stores for the first time.
