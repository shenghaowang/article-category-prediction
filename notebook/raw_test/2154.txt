The unemployment rate in Vermont has gone down to 3%, theVermont Department of Labor said.

The drop is one-tenth a percentage point since January.

Statewide, the unemployment rate ranged from a low of 2.4%in the Burlington and South Burlington Area, to a high of 7.1% in Derby.

Vermont has seen a decline in the number of unemployedresidents over the last seven years, Labor Commissioner Lindsay Kurrle said.

There was also a greater number of jobs in the economycompared to last year, according to state employers.
