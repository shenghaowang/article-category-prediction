U.K. mobile operator continues fixed-line push with Spanish cable acquisition; analyst predicts Italy, Netherlands could be next on Voda's list.

After months of speculation, Vodafone on Monday confirmed that it will acquire Spanish cable operator Ono for 7.2 billion, continuing its push to build up fixed assets in Europe.
