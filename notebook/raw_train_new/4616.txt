CHICAGO -- Grain futures were mostly higher Friday on the Chicago Board of Trade.

Wheat for Mar delivery was unchanged at $5.3275 a bushel; Mar corn was 7 cents higher at 3.87 a bushel; Mar oats were up 7 cents to $2.8175 a bushel; while Mar soybeans gained .75 cent to $9.9175 bushel.

Beef was mixed and pork was lower on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange.

Feb live cattle was up .25 cent at $1.5445 a pound; Mar feeder cattle fell .75 cent to $2.0485 a pound; while Feb lean hogs were 1.12 cents lower to $.7450 a pound.
