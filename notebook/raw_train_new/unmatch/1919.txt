FAYETTEVILLE, Ark. (KNWA) - The Veterans Health Care System of the Ozarks (VHSO) has announced a town hall meeting open to the public on October 29.

The town hall will serve as an open forum for veterans and a listening session for VHSO leadership. The goal is to have veterans, their families and beneficiaries have the opportunity to be heard and have their concerns addressed by senior VA officials.

VHSO will also give an update regarding Pathology Lookback.

The meeting will take place at Veterans Health Care System of the Ozarks, located at 1100 N. College Avenue in the auditorium of building 3 at 11 a.m.
