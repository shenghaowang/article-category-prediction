Here we go again Brits are headed to the polls for another crucial vote less than a year after the Brexit referendum. U.K voters will cast their ballots on Thursday June 8 in a snap or outofcycle general election called by Prime Minister Theresa May the leader of the Conservative Party just six weeks ago. At stake is which party will guide the U.K. through the upcoming divorce negotiations with Brussels which are expected to be tough and which can be expected to define the country for generations to come.
