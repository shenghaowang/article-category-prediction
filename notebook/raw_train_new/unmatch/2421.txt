A panel on one of the wings on a Delta aircraft fell off during a flight from Orlando, Fla., to Atlanta, on Sunday, according to the airline.

The detached panel did not impact the aircraft's ability to fly or land, Delta spokesman Anthony Black said of Flight 2412.
