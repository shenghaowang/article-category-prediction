The developer behind the hit smartphone game Candy Crush Saga expects to be worth as much as $7.6 billion when it floats later this month.

King Digital Entertainment, which recently moved its tax base to Ireland, said that it expected to price its US shares at between $21 and $24 when it started trading on the New York Stock Exchange this month.

The IPO will raise as much as $532.8 million (320 million), at the top-end of the planned range.

It planned to sell 15.3 million shares with a further 6.7 million shares offloaded by existing shareholders, including Apax Partners, Index Ventures and the executive team.
