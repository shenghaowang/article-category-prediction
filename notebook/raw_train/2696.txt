NEW YORK (Reuters) - U.S. stocks were little changed on Wednesday amid signs of progress in diplomatic attempts to ease tensions in Ukraine and as investors shrugged off concern over weakness in the Chinese economy.

The Dow Jones industrial average .DJI fell 11.49 points or 0.07 percent, to 16,339.76, the S&P 500 .SPX gained 0.53 points or 0.03 percent, to 1,868.16 and the Nasdaq Composite .IXIC added 16.144 points or 0.37 percent, to 4,323.332.
