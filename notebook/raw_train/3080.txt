Chiquita Brands International (CQB) announced Monday morning that it agreed to merge with Fyffes plc in a stock-for-stock transaction that is expected to result in Chiquita shareholders owning approximately 50.7% of ChiquitaFyffes, and Fyffes shareholders owning approximately 49.3%.

Chiquita Brands gapped open sharply higher Monday and is now up 1.61 at $12.45. The stock has jumped to nearly a 5-month high.
