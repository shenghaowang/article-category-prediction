Saudi Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Defense, held talks in Jeddah on Monday with Prime Minister of the Libyan National Accord government Fayez al-Sarraj, reported the Saudi Press Agency.

 

 The two officials discussed the latest developments in Libya and efforts exerted to restore security and stability there.

 

 The meeting was attended by Dr. Musaed bin Mohammed Al-Aiban, Minister of the State and Member of the Cabinet; Adel al-Jubeir, Minister of Foreign Affairs; Khalid bin Ali Al-Humaidan, Chief of the General Intelligence; Dr. Bandar bin Obaid Al-Rasheed, Secretary to the Crown Prince and a number of Libyan senior officials.
