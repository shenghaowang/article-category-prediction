Wheat for Jul was rose 8.75 cents at 5.1275 a bushel; Jul corn was up 8.25 cents at 3.5175 a bushel; Jul oats fell 4 cents at $2.5425 bushel; while Jul soybeans advanced 38.50 cents at $8.74 a bushel.

Beef was unchanged and pork was lower on the Chicago Mercantile Exchange. Aug. live cattle was unchanged at $1.0637 a pound; Aug. feeder cattle was lost .37 cent at 1.5220 a pound; while July lean hogs fell 1.93 cents at $.8137 a pound.
