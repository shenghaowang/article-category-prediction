Sun Pharmaceutical Industries Ltd has informed BSE regarding a Press Release dated March 13, 2014 titled "Import Alert for Karkhadi unit from US FDA".

Sun Pharmaceutical Industries announced an import alert from the USFDA for its cephalosporin facility located at Karkhadi, Gujarat in India. This import alert was issued by the USFDA as a follow up to the last inspection of the facility, during which some non-compliance of current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) regulations were identified.Source : BSE
