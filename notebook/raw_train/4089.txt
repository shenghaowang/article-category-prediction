A new business and private banking leadership team in Newcastle has been unveiled by Yorkshire Bank.

The team is based at Yorkshire Bank's Business and Private Banking Centre, Quayside. The centre is led by Simon Lenney and has access to Yorkshire Bank's 1bn Business Expansion Fund.

"This is a re-invigorated Yorkshire Bank. We have a strong and dynamic team, with experience across the commercial spectrum, and access to funding to support businesses and private individuals throughout the North East," said Lenney.

"We remain strongly committed to the North East where we support many successful and fast-growing enterprises and have done so for many years."

The centre is part of the bank's North East team which also has an office in High Street, Stockton.
