<p>March 12 (Reuters) - <a class="cite" href="loadEntitySearchResults('%2Fsearch%2Fentity%3FentityId%3Dhttp%253A%252F%252Fd.opencalais.com%252Fcomphash-1%252Fc36cc826-5c2f-3bef-b19f-093da883e690%26sort%3Ddate%26terms%3D%2522King%2520Digital%2520Entertainment%2520Plc%2522')" id="/search/entity?entityId=http%3A%2F%2Fd.opencalais.com%2Fcomphash-1%2Fc36cc826-5c2f-3bef-b19f-093da883e690&amp;sort=date&amp;terms=%22King%20Digital%20Entertainment%20Plc%22">King Digital Entertainment Plc</a>, the maker of hit mobile phone game Candy Crush Saga, expects to be worth up to $7.6 billion when it goes public this month as the Irish company looks to take advantage of strong demand for technology investments.</p>
<p>The successful IPO of Twitter Inc in November and a surge in Facebook Inc's share price have fueled speculation that a string of technology firms could come to market, including music-sharing service Spotify, lodging service <a class="cite" href="loadEntitySearchResults('/search/entity?entityId=http%3A%2F%2Fd.opencalais.com%2Fcomphash-1%2Fc5c25fdd-025d-322f-8d79-5f24e31c7088&amp;sort=date&amp;terms=&quot;AirBnB&quot;')" id="/search/entity?entityId=http%3A%2F%2Fd.opencalais.com%2Fcomphash-1%2Fc5c25fdd-025d-322f-8d79-5f24e31c7088&amp;sort=date&amp;terms=%22AirBnB%22">AirBnB</a> and payments company Square.</p>
<p>King should also get a boost in its U.S. initial public offering from a surge in shares of digital coupon company Coupons.com Inc after it went public on Friday. Shares of Coupons.com doubled after being priced above the company's planned IPO range.</p>
<p>King Digital said it expected to price its IPO of 22.2 million shares at between $21 and $24 per share. At the top of this range, the company would be valued at about $7.6 billion.</p>
<p>The offering is scheduled to be priced on March 25 and the stock will start trading on March 26, two underwriters told Reuters.</p>
