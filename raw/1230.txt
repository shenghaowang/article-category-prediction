<p>A Delta Air Lines plane flying from Orlando to Atlanta lost one of its wing panels while in midair. Nobody was injured, and the craft landed safely, <a href="/topics/cnn/">CNN</a> reported.</p>
<p>“Crew members aboard Flight 2412 reported an access panel had come off the plane’s wing while in flight,” said Anthony Black, a spokesman for the company, to <a href="/topics/cnn/">CNN</a>.</p>
<p>The Boeing 757 plane was carting 179 passengers and six crew members. It ultimately touched down safely at Atlanta’s Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport.</p>
<p>Safety officials are performing an inspection on the plane to determine why the panel blew off — a “highly unusual” occurrence, said Mr. Black, <a href="/topics/cnn/">CNN</a> reported.</p>
