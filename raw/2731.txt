<p><strong><em>Today's stock  market news, March 10, 2014: </em></strong>The <a href="http://www.moneymorning.com/tag/dow-jones-industrial-average" target="_blank">Dow Jones  Industrial Average</a> gained 0.19% on Friday to close at 16,452.72. The Nasdaq  lost 0.37%, closing at 4,336.22. The S&amp;P 500 remained flat, finishing at 1,878.04.</p>
<p><a href="https://moneymorning.com/tag/gold-prices/" target="_blank">Gold prices</a> fell 0.86% to $1,340.50 an ounce. <a href="https://moneymorning.com/tag/silver-prices/" target="_blank">Silver prices</a> slumped by  3.05% to close at $20.92 an ounce. West Texas Intermediate gained 1.00%,  trading at $102.58 a barrel. </p>
<p>What time is it? Remember, daylights savings time happened on Sunday  morning, springing clocks forward by an hour. Be sure to set those car clocks  and microwaves.</p>
