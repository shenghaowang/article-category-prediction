<p><strong>Bank of America Refinance Rates</strong></p>
<p>The benchmark 30-year refinance at Bank of America is advertised at 4.500% on Wednesday with an APR of 4.624%. The shorter-term 15 year refinance FRM has a quoted rate of 3.500% with an APR of 3.716%.</p>
<p>Bank of America also allows qualified borrowers to refinance into an adjustable rate mortgage. The 5-year refinance is quoted at 3.250% with an APR of 3.021%. A 10/1 ARM refinance is quoted at 3.875% with a corresponding APR of 3.543% today, while the 7/1 ARM has an advertised rate of 3.500% with a 3.194% APR.</p>
<p>Bank of America also offers VA loans, FHA loans, and jumbo loans. The rates for these loan products are not quoted daily. Qualified borrowers interested in these loans should contact a Bank of America loan specialist for a quote.</p>
<p>The purchase rates above assume a borrower with excellent credit buying an owner-occupied single-family home in California for $200,000 with a 20% down payment and an 80% LTV.</p>
