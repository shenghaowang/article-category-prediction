<p data-page="1">Pizza chain Sbarro has filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection for the second time in three years.</p>
<p>The tourist favorite has 1,000 to 5,000 creditors, $100 million to $500 million in assets and $100 million to $500 million in estimated liabilities, according to a document filed in Manhattan Federal Court.</p>
<p>The move comes as the Melville, L.I., company seeks to reorganize due to flagging sales. Sbarro recently announced the closure of about 150 restaurants.</p>
