<p>PITTSBURGH - DICK'S Sporting Goods Inc. (NYSE: DKS), the largest U.S. sporting goods retailer, reported sales and earnings results for the fourth quarter and full year exceeded the company's expectations.</p>
<p>Dick's reported consolidated net income of $138.6 million, or $1.11 per diluted share, for the 13 weeks ended Feb. 1, 2014, compared to the company's expectations provided on Nov. 19, 2013, of $1.04 to 1.07 per diluted share.</p>
<p>Unshifted consolidated same store sales for the fourth quarter increased 6.3 percent, compared to the company's guidance of an approximate 2 to 3 percent increase.</p>
<p>'We generated strong results in our fourth quarter, with record earnings per share of $1.11, above the upper end of the guidance range we provided in our third-quarter press release, as our sales and merchandise margin exceeded our expectations,' said Edward W. Stack, chairman and chief executive officer. 'As we look to 2014, we believe our robust and growing omni-channel network and exciting merchandising opportunities will support double-digit growth in earnings.'</p>
<p>In the fourth quarter, the company opened six new DICK'S Sporting Goods stores, one new True Runner store and closed three underperforming Golf Galaxy stores. The company also remodeled one DICK'S Sporting Goods store during the fourth quarter. As of Feb. 1, 2014, the company operated 558 DICK'S Sporting Goods stores in 46 states, with approximately 30.1 million square feet and 79 Golf Galaxy stores in 29 states, with approximately 1.4 million square feet.</p>
