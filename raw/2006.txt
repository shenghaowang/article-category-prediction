<p>SHELBYVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- A student was arrested at Shelby County High School Monday after he allegedly threatened another student with a knife. </p>
<p>According to a news release from Shelby County Public Schools, the student brought a knife to school Monday morning. When he threatened another student with it, adults and other students quickly got involved. </p>
<p>Police were called and the student was arrested. </p>
<p>Parents of the students who were directly involved were notified personally, and all other parents of high school students were contacted using an automated One Call Now system with a recorded message. </p>
<p>The recorded message from Principal Oakley stated there had been an incident, but officials had responded quickly and school was continuing normally. </p>
<p>A letter was also sent home to all parents.</p>
