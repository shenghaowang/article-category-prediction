<p>We are just over a week away from Call of Duty: Black Ops 4, and Treyarch is teasing us with tiny glimpses of Black Ops 4 maps. While Blackout is getting all the hype (deserved too), I can’t wait to jump back into the regular multiplayer. I’ve been on a Battle Royale kick since H1Z1’s heyday. It’ll be nice to jump back into a more structured multiplayer from time to time.</p>
<p><em><strong>UPDATE: Black Ops 4 will launch with 14 maps on Day 1. Nuketown in November will bring it to 15. Ten maps will be new, while four (five including Nuketown) will be remastered classics from the Black Ops franchise. Treyarch gave us a short look at Miltia. </strong></em></p>
