<p>Tom Cruise is back in the pilot seat, and this time he doesn’t need a wingman. No Goose. But he’s still dangerous in the upcoming American Made. Based on the exploits of Barry Seal, it tells the story of pilot hired by the CIA and DEA into one of the largest covert operations in US history.</p>
<p><iframe allow="autoplay; encrypted-media" allowfullscreen="" frameborder="0" height="675" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AEBIJRAkujM?feature=oembed" width="1200"></iframe></p>
<p>Can you go wrong with an action film featuring Tom Cruise? It helps the film reunites him with Doug Liman, who directed Edge of Tomorrow. Other cast members include Domhnall Gleeson, Sarah Wright, E. Roger Mitchell, Jesse Plemons, Lola Kirke, Alejandro Edda, Benito Martinez, Caleb Landry Jones and Jayma Mays.</p>
<p><img alt="Tom Cruise in American Made trailer" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-27133" height="720" sizes="(max-width: 1280px) 100vw, 1280px" src="https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made.jpg" srcset="https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made.jpg 1280w, https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made-800x450.jpg 800w, https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made-1160x653.jpg 1160w, https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made-293x165.jpg 293w, https://www.newsledge.com/wp-content/uploads/2017/06/tom-cruise-american-made-770x433.jpg 770w" width="1280"/></p>
<p>Expect the film to take a bit of creative license with the story, but the man’s life is insane. <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Barry_Seal" target="_blank">Reading over his Wikipedia entry</a>, you can see he was instrumental as an informant for the DEA and running covert ops for the CIA. He was an actual pilot, though he was fired from TWA. The trailer implies he was recruited from the airline.</p>
<p>From there he started running drugs for the Medellin Cartel earning up to $500,000 per flight. Pretty sure a commercial airline pilot isn’t pulling that in.</p>
