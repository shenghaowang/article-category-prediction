<p><b>American Airlines</b> (<a href="https://investorplace.com/stock-quotes/aa-stock-quote/" rel="nofollow">AA</a>) and <b>JetBlue</b> (<a href="https://investorplace.com/stock-quotes/jblu-stock-quote/" rel="nofollow">JBLU</a>) are ending a partnership that made traveling easier for some of its customers.</p>
