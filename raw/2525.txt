<p>Consumer shares were generally mixed in 
<a href="https://www.nasdaq.com/quotes/premarket.aspx">pre-market
trading</a>

Tuesday.</p>
<p>In consumer-sector news, Dick's Sporting Goods (
<a href="https://www.nasdaq.com/symbol/dks">DKS</a>

) shares were up about 2% in pre-market trading, recovering from an
earlier loss, after reporting fiscal Q4 earnings that were in line
with expectations even as its guidance for Q1 and FY 2014 came in
below estimates. DKS's 52-week range is between $45.00 and
$58.87.</p>
<p>Bon-Ton Stores (
<a href="https://www.nasdaq.com/symbol/bont">BONT</a>

) shares fell 5.7% to $10.25 pre-market, after the retailer said
president and CEO Brendan Hoffmann has notified the board that he
won't renew his contract when it expires on Feb. 7, 2015 due to
personal reasons. BONT fell 5.7% pre-market to $10.25, nearing the
low end of its 52-week range between $9.12 and $22.68.</p>
