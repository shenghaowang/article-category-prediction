<p>Chinese Premier Li Keqiang has said "we will not give up on any suspected clue that has been found" as the search for the missing Malaysia Airlines flight MH370 continues.</p>
<p>Three satellite images have been released on a Chinese government website.</p>
<p>They show what appear to be large, floating objects in the South China Sea. Previous sightings of possible debris have proved fruitless.</p>
