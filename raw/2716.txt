Chief digital officer Adam Brotman said that the company is actively working on making mobile ordering a reality.</p>
<p>“It’s something our customers have asked for,” he said. “We’re not revealing a lot of details about it now.”</p>
<p>Starbucks processes about 5 million mobile transactions a week. The company’s working on expanding and improving its rewards program to boost sales.</p>
<p>SBUX stock is up about 0.8% Wednesday.</p>
