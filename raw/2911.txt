<p class="description">American Eagle Outfitters (AEO) operates as a retailer that engages in the design, marketing, and sale of clothing in the United States and Canada. The company offers jeans and graphic Ts, as well as accessories, outerwear, footwear, basics, and swimwear. As of February 1, 2014, American Eagle Outfitters, Inc. operated 1143 stores comprising 944 American Eagle Outfitters stores, 122 Aerie stand-alone stores, and 77 MARTIN + OSA stores. In addition, it offers its products through its websites. The company was founded in 1972 and is headquartered in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. AEO is affected by changing consumer preferences and fashion trends, as well as raw material and labor price increases. AEO has been paying dividends since 2004, and last cut its dividend in 2013. AEO pays its dividends quarterly.
