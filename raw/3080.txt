<p> Chiquita Brands International (<a href="http://www.rttnews.com/SymbolSearch.aspx?Symbol=CQB" runat="server" style="text-decoration:none;">CQB</a>) announced Monday morning that it agreed to merge with Fyffes plc in a stock-for-stock transaction that is expected to result in Chiquita shareholders owning approximately 50.7% of ChiquitaFyffes, and Fyffes shareholders owning approximately 49.3%.</p>
<p>Chiquita Brands gapped open sharply higher Monday and is now up 1.61 at $12.45. The stock has jumped to nearly a 5-month high. </p>
