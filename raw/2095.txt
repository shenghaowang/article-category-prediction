<p>LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- Price Less Foods is opening a new store in Louisville on Dixie Highway and is holding several job fairs this week for potential workers. </p>
<p>According to a news release, the fourth Louisville-area Price Less Foods store will be located at 10986 Dixie Highway and is set to open April 24, 2014. </p>
<p>A job fair will be held at the Dixie Highway location starting Wednesday, March 12 and continuing through Friday, March 14 from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. each day. </p>
<p>Price Less Foods has full-time and part-time openings and is hiring for all positions ranging from store management to customer service associates. </p>
