<p><strong>FTC probing  Herbalife</strong></p>
<p>The Federal Trade Commission opened a formal investigation into Herbalife’s operations yesterday, pushing shares of the nutrition and weight loss company sharply lower.</p>
<p>Shares of Herbalife plunged as much as  15 percent after being temporarily halted but gradually recovered from lows, ending the day down 7.3 percent. Shares were up more than 4 percent prior to the halt. A circuit breaker also briefly halted trading in shares of rival Nu Skin due to volatility.</p>
