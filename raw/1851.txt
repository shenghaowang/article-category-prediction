<p> <em>Check out which companies are making headlines before the bell</em>: </p>
<p><a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="15235" data-inline-quote-symbol="DKS" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=DKS" target="_self">  Dick's Sporting Goods</a>–The sporting goods retailer earned $1.11 per share for the fourth quarter, matching estimates, with sales slightly above estimates. Dick's had raised its fourth quarter guidance on February 10, based on improving sales.</p>
<p><a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="17284" data-inline-quote-symbol="M" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=M" target="_self">  Macy's</a>–The retailer's shares were upgraded to "outperform" from "market perform" at Wells Fargo, which points to a solid performance by Macy's in what was a challenging holiday season for the industry.</p>
<p><a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="35602" data-inline-quote-symbol="URBN" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=URBN" target="_self">  Urban Outfitters</a>–Urban Outfitters reported a fourth quarter profit of 59 cents per share, five cents above estimates, though sales were short of consensus. The clothing retailer also issued a cautious outlook for the current quarter.</p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="14899" data-inline-quote-symbol="DD-MX" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=DD-MX" target="_self">DuPont</a> –DuPont said <a href="/2014/02/25/harsh-weather-tests-optimism-over-us-economy.html" target="_self">severe winter weather</a> as well as the <a href="/2014/03/11/ukraine-raising-new-national-guard-slams-russia.html" target="_self">rising tension in Ukraine</a> will impact current quarter earnings and sales. DuPont has a factory in Ukraine that produces a variety of seeds. Despite those issues, the company is standing by its 2014 earnings guidance of $4.20 - $4.45 per share. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="22061" data-inline-quote-symbol="JCP" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=JCP" target="_self">J.C. Penney</a>–The troubled retailer was upgraded to "buy" from "neutral" at Citi, which said it believes the retailer can continue to deliver positive comparable store sales as it updates its merchandise mix and implements other changes. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="8016" data-inline-quote-symbol="AAPL" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=AAPL" target="_self">Apple</a>–Apple was upgraded to "outperform" from "sector perform" at Pacific Crest Securities, based on its ability to generate cash and the potential for a higher priced iPhone 6 to spur sales in 2015. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="12432" data-inline-quote-symbol="CASY" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=CASY" target="_self">Casey's General</a>–Casey's fell 11 cents short of estimates with fiscal third quarter profit of 38 cents per share, with revenue shy of consensus as well. The convenience store operator cites higher costs. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="12226" data-inline-quote-symbol="BYD" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=BYD" target="_self">Boyd Gaming</a>–Elliott Associates has taken a 4.99 percent stake in the casino operator. In an SEC filing, Elliott said shares of Boyd are undervalued and represent an attractive investment opportunity. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="17226" data-inline-quote-symbol="FCEL" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=FCEL" target="_self">FuelCell</a>–FuelCell matched estimates with a first quarter loss of four cents per share, with revenue slightly above forecasts. Despite the loss, the maker of environmentally friendly power systems is seeing increased sales, and its stock has more than tripled this year. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="34172" data-inline-quote-symbol="TGT" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=TGT" target="_self">Target</a>–The retailer was <a href="/2014/03/11/-target-claims-it-copied-watch-designs.html" target="_self">sued by Swatch</a>, which accuses the retailer of selling watches that copy its designs. Target declined comment on the lawsuit but did say its policy is to respect the intellectual property rights of others. </p>
<p> <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="56338" data-inline-quote-symbol="TMUS" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=TMUS" target="_self">T-Mobile US</a>, <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="143380" data-inline-quote-symbol="S" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=S" target="_self">Sprint </a>– <a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="7949" data-inline-quote-symbol="9984.T-JP" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=9984.T-JP" target="_self">Softbank</a> CEO Masayoshi Son told the Charlie Rose show he would <a href="/2014/02/05/sprint-eyes-45-billion-in-financing-for-t-mobile-report.html" target="_self">like to buy T-Mobile</a> and merge it with Softbank's Sprint operation, despite indications that U.S. <a href="/2014/02/04/fcc-skeptical-on-sprint-t-mobile-deal.html" target="_self">regulators are against it</a>.</p>
<p><a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="25619" data-inline-quote-symbol="MSFT" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=MSFT" target="_self">  Microsoft</a>–Microsoft will launch its "Titanfall" game for its Xbox One videogame console today, <a href="/2014/01/07/ps4-sales-top-4-million-leaving-xbox-one-in-the-dust.html" target="_self">hoping to boost sales</a> for the latest Xbox version.</p>
<p><a class="inline_quotes" data-gdsid="23646" data-inline-quote-symbol="LJPC" href="//www.cnbc.com/quotes/?symbol=LJPC" target="_self">  La Jolla Pharmaceuticals</a>–The drug maker saw positive results in a mid-stage study for its experimental drug to treat chronic kidney disease. </p>
