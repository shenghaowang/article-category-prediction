<p><strong>The city of Los Angeles is suing Time Warner Cable for unpaid cable network fees. </strong></p>
<p>Los Angeles filed the lawsuit on Friday claiming that TWC was cheating on its franchise fees to the city.</p>
<p>According to the report, TWC owes a total of $9.7 million for franchise fees and public, education and governmental channel fees from 2008 to 2011.</p>
<p>The report also stated that TWC had received $500 million  year from using the city of Los Angeles’ cable system and that the company “blatantly refused to live up to its obligations to the city.”</p>
<p>Los Angeles typically charges cable companies 5% of their revenue instead of charging them rent in order to use the cities wires and cable boxes.</p>
<p>TWC continues to deny the allegations made by the city.</p>
