<p>NEW YORK, NY--(Marketwired - Mar 16, 2014) -  ALIBABA IPO: Exclusive New Details - leading private-companies research firm PrivCo confirms with sources close to China Internet giant ALIBABA's record-breaking IPO plans that the following are likely to be announced imminently:</p>
<ul style="list-style-type: disc">
<li><strong>IPO TO LIST IN THE U.S.:</strong> "Now 100% certain," in blow to orig. front-runner Hong Kong
            </li>
<li><strong>STOCK EXCHANGE: </strong>The New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) favored to beat out the Tech-Heavy NASDAQ
            </li>
<li><strong>IPO SIZE: </strong>$20 BILLION (Largest IPO In History)
            </li>
<li><strong>ALIBABA IPO VALUATION: </strong>$150 BILLION (Also New Record)
            </li>
<li><strong>BANKERS: </strong>6 Firms Now In Line to Co-Lead Historic IPO: GOLDMAN SACHS, MORGAN STANLEY, CITIGROUP, JP MORGAN, CREDIT SUISSE &amp; DEUTSCHE BANK. (An underwriting syndicate led by all 6 banks would be an "IPO dream team" given their complementary strengths, PrivCo's analysis concludes.)
            </li>
<li><strong>IPO LAW FIRM: </strong>SIMPSON THACHER (the elite New York law firm)
            </li>
<li><strong>VALUATION MULTIPLES (PrivCo Analysis): </strong>22x Revenue and a P/E Ratio of 54 (PrivCo Data on ALIBABA's Internal Financials)
            </li>
<li>"While the $150 Billion IPO valuation for ALIBABA might give some investors vertigo at first glance, PrivCo data concludes that 22x Revenue and a P/E of 54 is actually in line with other recent Internet IPOs," says PrivCo Founder &amp; CEO Sam Hamadeh, an IPO expert and corporate finance lawyer.
            </li>
<li><strong>"THIS IS A BIG WIN FOR THE U.S. Over Hong Kong, </strong>and a welcome boost for Wall Street and for New York's financial sector," Hamadeh adds.</li>
</ul>
