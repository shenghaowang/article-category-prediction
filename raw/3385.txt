According to our observations, the 30-year refinance loan retained its rate level on Monday, as it’s still offered at rate of 4.500%. The same can be said about the shorter-term, but equally popular 15-year FRM, as the opening rate is coming out at 3.625%. Individuals, who prefer refinancing with non-conventional loans, may be interested to learn that the 30-year fixed FHA-backed loan is currently offered at a rate of 3.625%. Adjustable rate loans could be appealing to borrowers, who prefer flexible interest rate conditions. Presently, the 5-year ARM is listed by Wells Fargo at a rate of 3.250%. The 5-year ARM FHA is published at 3.250%, according to the lender’s rate information. Additional details on today’s mortgage interest rates and APRs can be found below.</p>
