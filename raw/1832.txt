<p>March 11 (Reuters) - Men’s Wearhouse Inc said it would acquire rival Jos. A. Bank Clothiers Inc for about $1.8 billion, ending a  five-month merger saga that started with Jos. A. Bank offering to buy its larger menswear rival. </p>
<p>The increased offer price of $65 per share represents a premium of 5.1 percent to Jos. A. Bank’s Monday closing price.     (Reporting by Siddharth Cavale in Bangalore; Editing by Saumyadeb Chakrabarty)</p>
