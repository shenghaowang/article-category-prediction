<p>SEYMOUR, Ind. (WDRB) -- Lane restrictions along Interstate 65 south of Seymour begin at 6 p.m. on Sunday. The pavement renovation project will continue through November. </p>
<p>Indiana Department of Transportation officials anticipate lane closures and restrictions on a weekly basis. The closures will begin at 6 p.m. Sundays and extend to Fridays at 1 p.m.</p>
<p>This week&amp;aposs work focuses on forming median crossovers to shift traffic based on construction activity</p>
<p>Southbound motorists will be impacted between mile markers 47 and 45, as well as 43 and 38. </p>
