<p><strong>CEO Commentary</strong></p>
<p id="">Edward W. Stack, Chairman and CEO of DKS commented: “We generated strong results in our fourth quarter, with record earnings per share of $1.11, above the upper end of the guidance range we provided in our third quarter press release, as our sales and merchandise margin exceeded our expectations. As we look to 2014, we believe our robust and growing omni-channel network and exciting merchandising opportunities will support double-digit growth in earnings.”</p>
