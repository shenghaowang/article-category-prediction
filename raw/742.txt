<p>U.S. consumers began this month feeling more worried about their economic future, according to one survey of households released Friday.</p>
<p>The <strong>Thomson-Reuters/University of Michigan</strong> preliminary March sentiment index unexpectedly slipped to 79.9 from an end-February reading of 81.6, according to an economist who has seen the numbers.</p>
