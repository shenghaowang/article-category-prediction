<p><a href="https://www.geeky-gadgets.com/hackers-accuse-mt-gox-bitcoin-of-fraud-after-hack-10-03-2014/">Mt. Gox</a> recently filed for bankruptcy after the company said that they had been hacked and over $400 million worth of Bitcoins had been stolen.</p>
<p>We heard a report earlier today that MT. Gox was hacked recently, and the hackers who carrier out the attack obtained data from Mt. Gox.</p>
<p><img alt="bitcoin" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-169498" height="533" sizes="(max-width: 700px) 100vw, 700px" src="https://www.geeky-gadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bitcoin.jpg" srcset="https://www.geeky-gadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bitcoin.jpg 700w, https://www.geeky-gadgets.com/wp-content/uploads/2014/03/bitcoin-300x228.jpg 300w" width="700"/><span id="more-169496"></span></p>
<p>The hackers alleged that Mt. Gox are still in possession of the Bitcoins that the company previously reported as stolen, Mt. Gox has neither confirmed of denied this.</p>
<p>Now ZDNet has discovered that Mt. Gox has also filed for bankruptcy in the US, the company previously filed for bankruptcy in Japan.</p>
<p>According to the ZDNet report, the company filed chapter 15 bankruptcy in the US yesterday, this is the second country that the company has made this filing in.</p>
<p>From what has been revealed so far, it looks like there may be a little more to the story of Mt. Gox than originally thought, as soon as we get some more information on exactly what happened, we will let you guys know.</p>
