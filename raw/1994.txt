With regards to current refinance rates at this mortgage provider, the benchmark 30-year FRM is published at a rate of 4.500%. The 15-year fixed refinance loan could be appealing to some mortgage shoppers as it still carries 3.625% in interest. Borrowers, who are not eligible for conventional loans, may want to take a look at FHA-insured mortgages. These type of loans usually come with lower down payment conditions than standard conventional mortgages. Currently, the 30-year fixed FHA mortgage is available at a rate of 4.250%. Turning attention to the lender’s adjustable rate loan offerings, the 5-year ARM is quoted at a rate of 3.250%. Another flexible refinance loan option is the 5-year ARM FHA, which is published at a rate of 3.250% on Tuesday. For additional information on Wells Fargo’s latest refinance rates, as well as details on home purchase loan rates, please check the data below.</p>
<p>Wells Fargo offers excellent mortgage interest rates under both its home purchase and refinance programs, so those potential borrowers who are looking to invest either in a new / used home or to refinance an existing loan, may find some ideal mortgage offerings at this lender.</p>
