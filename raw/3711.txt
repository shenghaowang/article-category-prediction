<p>LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- A special delivery is on its way to Bardstown.<br/><br/>A custom-built playhouse will be delivered to the son of fallen Bardstown Police officer Jason Ellis.</p>
<p>We were there when Hunter Ellis was given his new playhouse at the Home, Garden and Remodeling Show at the Kentucky Expo Center. Several local charities worked together to make it happen.<br/></p>
<p>Officer Ellis was gunned down last May, and no arrests have been made in the case.</p>
