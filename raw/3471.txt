<p>Some 319,000 people live in Wirral. Home to the world-famous Cammell Laird shipyard and Tranmere Rovers FC, the peninsula's golfing scene is also a major draw for visitors. Wirral boasts 1,900 listed buildings, while Birkenhead Park was the inspiration for New York's Central Park. Modern-day Wirral combines the area's stunning coastal views with key industries including hi-tech companies and major retailers.</p>
