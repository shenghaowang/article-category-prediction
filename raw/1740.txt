<p>Hong Kong may have lost out on Alibaba Group Holding Ltd.’s listing to New York, but Chinese technology companies continue to come to the city.</p>
<p>BAIOO Family Interactive Ltd., has started  gauging investor appetite for its initial public offering which could raise between $200 million and $250 million, according to a term sheet seen by Wall Street Journal Monday, in what could be the largest Chinese Internet firm listing in the city since 2007.</p>
