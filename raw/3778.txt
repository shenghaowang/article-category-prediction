<p>LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- Pizza chain Sbarro has filed for bankruptcy.<br/></p>
<p>The move came less than three weeks after it closed more than 40 percent of its U.S. stores. The company says the bankruptcy is part of a reorganization plan with creditors that hold 98 percent of its debt.<br/></p>
<p>It&amp;aposs expected to help get rid of about $140 million of debt and move the company forward.<br/></p>
<p>This is the second time it&amp;aposs filed for bankruptcy in less than three years.</p>
