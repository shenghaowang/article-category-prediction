Luckily, for borrowers, Tuesday’s improvements haven’t evaporated a day later, as the updated rate sheet shows no changes in mortgage interest rates. On the other hand, annual percentage rates (APRs) moved lower on some of the refinance loan packages. Currently, the Pittsburgh-headquartered top loan provider advertises the 30-year mortgage loan, for refinancing purposes, at a rate of 4.500% – 4.750%. The starting rate on the 20-year refinance mortgage is hovering at 4.250% – 4.500%, according to the most up-to-date information from the lender. Borrowers, who prefer refinancing their existing loans over shorter-term, may be interested to learn that the 15-year FRM is published at a rate of 3.375% – 3.875% at PNC Bank. With regards to the 10-year fixed rate loan, it can be locked in at a rate of 3.125% – 3.750%. The bank also updated its home loan rates for today, check the details on them below.</p>
<p>PNC Bank has been offering excellent mortgage rates under both its home purchase and refinance programs, so those who are looking to invest either in a new / used home or to refinance an existing loan, may find some ideal mortgage solutions at this bank.</p>
