<p> <strong>Russian</strong> troops <a class="icon none" href="http://www.bloomberg.com/news/2014-03-12/ukraine-warns-of-russian-troop-buildup-as-premier-to-meet-obama.html?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">are massing</a> along <strong>Ukraine's</strong> eastern border. <strong>Crimea</strong> is nearly <a class="icon none" href="http://www.marketwatch.com/story/g7-to-demand-russia-stops-its-push-to-annex-crimea-2014-03-12?link=MW_latest_news&amp;mod=article_inline" target="_blank">annexed</a> as a Russian territory. Stocks in <strong>Germany</strong> and <strong>France</strong> lost more than 1% as traders realize that, yes, geopolitics can matter for financial markets.</p>
<p>Meanwhile, in <strong>Asia</strong>, more investors are noticing <strong>China's</strong> slowing economic growth and its shadow-banking crisis. The price of <strong>copper</strong> is down again as traders <a class="icon none" href="http://www.theguardian.com/business/2014/mar/12/copper-sell-off-china-bond-default-market-four-year-low?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">fear defaults</a> after the country experienced its first domestic bond default.</p>
<p>What does that spell for gold? After <a class="icon none" href="https://www.google.com/search?hl=en&amp;gl=us&amp;tbm=nws&amp;authuser=0&amp;q=gold+four+month+high&amp;mod=article_inline" target="_blank">nearing</a> a fourth-month high on Tuesday, a gain, naturally.</p>
<p>Investors Wednesday are seeking out investments tied to the metal, which, a 28% 2013 slump notwithstanding, often serves as a safety play. The most actively traded gold futures contract is ahead by 1.3% to $1,365 per ounce in New York.</p>
<p> <strong>SPDR Gold Trust</strong> (<a class="icon none" href="http://quotes.barrons.com/gld?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">GLD</a>) is ahead by 1.3% in early trading, while <strong>Market Vectors Gold Miners ETF</strong> (<a class="icon none" href="http://quotes.barrons.com/gdx?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">GDX</a>) is ahead by 1.5%. The leveraged <strong>Direxion Daily Gold Miners Bull 3X Shares</strong> (<a class="icon none" href="http://quotes.barrons.com/nugt?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">NUGT</a>) and <strong>Direxion Daily Gold Miners Bear 3X Shares</strong> (<a class="icon none" href="http://quotes.barrons.com/dust?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">DUST</a>) are moving bynearly 5% apiece and <strong>iShares Silver Trust</strong> (<a class="icon none" href="http://quotes.barrons.com/slv?mod=article_inline" target="_blank">SLV</a>) is rising 1.1%.</p>
