<p><strong>Wells Fargo Refinance Rates</strong></p>
<p>The best refinance deals at Wells Fargo today include a 30-year fixed mortgage at 4.500% with an APR of 4.586%. An FHA fixed rate mortgage is quoted at 4.25% with a corresponding APR of 5.837%. 30-year jumbo refinances are also available with a published rate of 4.25% with an APR of 4.278%.</p>
<p>Larger conforming 30-year refinance loans in eligible areas have a quoted rate of 4.500% with an APR of 4.543%, with an FHA version available at 4.25% and a higher APR of 5.788%.</p>
<p>The popular 15-year refinance is advertised at 3.625% with a corresponding APR of 3.772%. Wells Fargo also allows homeowners to refinance into adjustable rate mortgages. The 5-year refinance ARM is quoted at 3.25% with an APR of 3.001%. A jumbo 5-year refinance ARM is advertised at 2.875% with an APR of 2.819%. This is the lowest mortgage rate advertised at the lender.</p>
<p>These rates are subject to change and are based on the applicant’s qualifications and the home type and location. Quoted APRs include discount points, origination fees, average loan fees and closing costs. To learn more about mortgage rates at Wells Fargo, please visit the lender’s website.</p>
