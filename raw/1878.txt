<p><a href="http://southerndailypress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bbt-bank.jpg"><img alt="Mortgage Rates Update: BB&amp;T Fixed, VA and FHA Mortgage Rates Roundup for March 11" class="alignright wp-image-14113" height="231" sizes="(max-width: 152px) 100vw, 152px" src="http://southerndailypress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bbt-bank-201x300.jpg" srcset="http://southerndailypress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bbt-bank-60x90.jpg 60w, http://southerndailypress.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/bbt-bank-16x25.jpg 16w" width="152"/></a>Mortgage rates on conventional home purchase and refinance loans remained firm at Branch Banking and Trust (NYSE:BBT) on Tuesday, March 11. Following the improvements on mortgage rates on 10-year home loans a day earlier, this type of loan retained its level today. The aforementioned home loan is listed at a rate of 3.000%. Home purchase loans which come with 15-year term are advertised at a rate of 3.375% at this lender. Moving up on the scale, BB&amp;T’s 20-year FRM is currently published at a rate of 4.125%, unchanged compared to yesterday’s rate quote. With regards to the long-term, 30-year conventional home loan, it remained stable at 4.375%, according to BB&amp;T’s latest data.</p>
<p>Switching to the lender’s non-conventional home loan offerings, today we noticed some lower rates on long-term VA and FHA-insured mortgages. Both the 30-year fixed FHA loan and the 30-year VA mortgage start at 3.750%, a downtick compared to the previous 3.875% that they carried on Monday. For further details on today’s home purchase rates, as well as information on current refinance rates, please check the data below.</p>
<p>Branch Banking and Trust has been offering excellent mortgage rates under both its home purchase and refinance programs, so those who are looking to invest either in a new / used home or to refinance an existing loan, may find some suitable mortgage deals at this lender.</p>
