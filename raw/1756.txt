<p class="desc">Zeidan's government has <a href="https://www.google.jo/url?sa=t&amp;rct=j&amp;q=&amp;esrc=s&amp;source=web&amp;cd=5&amp;cad=rja&amp;uact=8&amp;ved=0CEwQFjAE&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.albawaba.com%2Fnews%2Flibya-under-control-554546&amp;ei=hisfU4LND4iN7QbKsoHYAg&amp;usg=AFQjCNFYGkbSj6GELCwmV7SrqBWJ411Vog&amp;bvm=bv.62788935,d.ZGU" target="_blank" title="libya zeidan">received extensive criticism from both the parliament and the public for its failure to calm chaos and control rebel militias throughout the restive North African country</a> since taking office after the oust of former dictator Moammar Gaddafi in 2011.</p>
<p class="desc">The latest showcase of his leadership failure was the ability for eastern rebel groups to <a href="https://www.google.jo/url?sa=t&amp;rct=j&amp;q=&amp;esrc=s&amp;source=web&amp;cd=5&amp;cad=rja&amp;uact=8&amp;ved=0CD4QFjAE&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.reuters.com%2Farticle%2F2014%2F03%2F08%2Fus-libya-oil-idUSBREA2709K20140308&amp;ei=5SsfU_LZJNLo7Aah1ICgBQ&amp;usg=AFQjCNHsgNXRgHrh361gd98Uy7Bp709ONQ&amp;bvm=bv.62788935,d.ZGU" target="_blank" title="libya oil">use a ship called The Morning Glory to independently export at least 234,000 barrels of crude.</a> The<a href="https://www.google.jo/url?sa=t&amp;rct=j&amp;q=&amp;esrc=s&amp;source=web&amp;cd=2&amp;cad=rja&amp;uact=8&amp;ved=0CCwQqQIwAQ&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.aljazeera.com%2Fnews%2Fafrica%2F2014%2F03%2Ftanker-rebel-oil-libya-2014311133113876148.html&amp;ei=OiwfU-GiGZKA7Qaz9IDYBQ&amp;usg=AFQjCNFO3IJkQZa7o1IyC5ReuXBLYw9kZQ&amp;bvm=bv.62788935,d.ZGU" target="_blank" title="libya oil"> vessel managed to escape to sea from Al Sidra earlier Tuesday </a>even after Zeidan's government threatened arm action and air strikes to keep the tanker from reaching international waters.</p>
<p class="desc"><span>Zeidan, who was also previously abducted himself by rebel militias, has faced motions in parliament to resign in previous months. However, the General National Congress did not achieve the statutory quorum of 120 of its 194 members in the pass to implement the motion accordingly. </span></p>
