<p>Bank of England Governor Mark Carney pledged to go "above and beyond" the standards expected of the financial-services industry as it seeks to maintain the central bank's integrity in light of its links to a sprawling investigation into possible currency-market manipulation.</p>
<p>The governor was called to testify to the UK's Treasury Select Committee after the UK central bank suspended one of its employees in relation to the probe into currency-trading practices last week.</p>
