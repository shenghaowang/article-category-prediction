<p>The parent company of Boston Store announced Tuesday that its CEO will resign in early 2015.</p>
<p>Brendan L. Hoffman notified the company's Board of Directors that he will not renew his contract with Bon-Ton. His term expires in February of 2015.</p>
<p>Hoffman said he is resigning for personal reasons.</p>
<p>In a statement, Hoffman said "I have made the difficult decision to end my tenure with the company for strictly personal reasons."</p>
<p>Bon-Ton maintains dual corporate headquarters in Milwaukee and in York, Penn.</p>
<p>"While I regret that Brendan will be leaving Bon-Ton when his contract expires, I respect his decision," said Tim Grumbacher, Chairman of the Board for Bon-Ton. "The advance notice Brendan has given shoudl afford the company an opportunity to find and attract a talented leader to assume the role and ensure a smooth transition."</p>
